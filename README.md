# Virtual Reality Engine · 虚拟现实引擎

A civilisation-scale, bilingual (English / 中文) atlas of virtual reality — beginning from the premise that perception is already a world the brain renders from sparse signals, and that VR is that same machinery fed a different input. It traces immersion from the cave wall to spatial computing, through full-dive neural VR, AI-generated worlds, avatars and digital identity, virtual economies and societies, and simulation theory — toward the thesis that civilisation, having engineered matter and then information, is now turning to engineer experience itself.

Ten interconnected systems, a flagship immersion dial, an immersion-history timeline, an eight-axis immersive-civilisation radar, a six-voice analyst, a recursive epoch engine, and a dozen bespoke interactive visualizations — each careful to distinguish shipping technology from research frontier from marked speculation.
